[[Blocked|https://play.fancade.com/5C1AA5FCBA847DF9]] is a puzzle game by [[Martin Magni]]. The difficulty of 51 puzzles is incremental, from the easiest to the provably hardest!

## How to Play

Drag to move a car. Cars only move along the longer side. Unblock the red block and get it to the right to win!

## Solutions

[[/uploads/blocked.png]]

In the following notation, horizontal cells are marked from a to f, up-to-down. Vertical cells are numbered 1 to 6, left-to-right. "a1" is the most top-left corner. "b1<<S>>" means to slide the car found in the coordinate b1 down until it hits a wall or another car.  
If there is a number enclosed in parentheses, it means to move it in that direction an amount of times, e.g. "d2<<N>>(3)" means to move the car found in d2 up three times. Some shortest solutions are obtainable in this form.
