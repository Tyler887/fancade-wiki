Negates a [[number]] (or inverses the sign).

[[/uploads/negate.png]]

Inputs:

- Num: The number to negate.

Outputs:

- -Num: The negated number.

## Examples
- The top one turns `5` into `-5` while the last one turns `-8` into `8`. Positive numbers will become negative, and vice versa.
